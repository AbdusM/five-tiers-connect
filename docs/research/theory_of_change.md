## Strategic Roadmap
Based on this framework, the platform should evolve from "Appointment Booking" to a holistic **"Reentry Success Hub"**.

**Next Priorities:**
1.  **Resource Hub Expansion:** Expand specific sections for Housing, Legal Aid, and Education under a consolidated "Resources" tab (Addressing the "Resource Desert" problem).
2.  **Impact Metrics:** Enhance the Analytics dashboard to track "Stability Indicators" (consistent check-ins, voucher usage) and **"Community Connections"** (resource access).
